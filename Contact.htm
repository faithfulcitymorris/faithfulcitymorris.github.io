Contacts for Faithful City Morris Men

If you are interested in booking FCMM to dance at your event contact our Bagman (equivalent to secretary).

If you are interested in joining the side (beginners welcome) then contact our Squire (equivalent to chairman), or come along to the Talbot at Knightwick any Wednesday between mid September and the end of April at 8.00pm.
