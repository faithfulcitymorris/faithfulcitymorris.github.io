Faithful City Morris Kit

Our kit is a blue tabard with the Worcester Black Pear on the front and a pear tree by the River Severn on the back. This is worn over a white shirt with black breeches, white socks, black shoes and of course a pint of beer in the right hand!

 
We wear black top hats, and when a dancer has achieved a reasonable standard of dance, he wears a chequered green and blue ribbon around his hat, as shown here.

Our Fool (Richard Hannah) wears a multi-coloured rag jacket (as seen on the left). He is ably assisted by his Hobby Horse "Ned", who thrives on a diet of loose change generously provided by the audience.
